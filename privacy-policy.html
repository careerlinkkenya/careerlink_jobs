Privacy Policy

At CareerLink, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Data Collected

  • Name, phone number, email, and password during sign up
  • Job preferences and qualifications during profile setup
  • Application and payment data (e.g., uploaded receipts)

2. Use of Data

We use your information to:

  • Process job applications
  • Match you with suitable jobs
  • Contact you about job opportunities

3. Data Sharing

We only share data with trusted employers and payment verification partners. We do not sell or rent user data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is securely stored using Firebase and encrypted where necessary.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Contact us via careerlinkke@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve user experience and track anonymous usage statistics.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use implies agreement to the latest version.
